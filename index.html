About Me

Hi, I'm Deepthi Munagapati!

I'm a Computer Science student at Georgia Tech, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Information Internetworks. I’m passionate about building scalable AI systems and making technology more intelligent, accessible, and impactful.

Currently, I’m a Machine Learning Intern at Gabriel Money, where I’m building “Gabi,” a generative AI-powered financial chatbot that leverages retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) and FAISS/Pinecone to surface relevant insights from over 100K documents. I also implemented guardrails to improve factual accuracy and containerized services with Docker on AWS ECS.

Beyond that, I’ve contributed to bioinformatics tooling as a Software Engineer Intern at the Molecular Evolution Core, redesigning internal lab systems using React and Tailwind. I'm also conducting neural architecture search research with the VIP program at Georgia Tech, applying evolutionary algorithms and GNNs for object detection.

Some of my side projects include an arbitrage sports betting bot, a personalized course planner, and DevBot–an AI-powered assistant that provides real-time coding help to beginner programmers.

I’m always looking to connect with people building at the intersection of AI, systems, and real-world impact. Let’s connect and explore how we can collaborate!
